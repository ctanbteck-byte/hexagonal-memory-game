HEX MEMORY DUEL
Neural Pattern Recognition & Memory Combat
⬡ Mission Briefing
  • 19 hexagons arranged in a large hexagonal formation are each assigned a number (1–9).
  • You have 60 seconds to memorise the layout before numbers are replaced with letters A–S.
  • Each round reveals a target number. Find 3 adjacent hexagons in a straight line whose hidden numbers sum to that target.
  • Submit 3 letters in sequence. Correct → +1 point. Wrong → −1 point.
  • Rounds last 30 seconds of inactivity. Game ends after 10 rounds. Highest score wins.
  • You play against ARIA — the AI opponent who memorised the grid perfectly.
